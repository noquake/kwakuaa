    Celeste Ng

    Everything I Never Told You (2014)

    Rachel recommended this book to me. It's her favorite. We went out to Barnes and Nobles on our first date and we bought it there. Once I was home I finished half of the book in like two nights. Not only was the story gripping but I feel like in this one the characters were more enrapturing. I found myself becoming so invested in their own development that I didn't even want the book to end.

    If I had any special comments it'd have to be about the youngest daughter Hannah. It pains me so much that even within her own family she was an outsider. Hannah died and the whole family was just trying to replace her with Hannah, the other daughter. The mother especially, consciously shoving her dreams onto Lydia. Such a difficult read — my heart was aching the entire latter half of the novel. It hit hard.
