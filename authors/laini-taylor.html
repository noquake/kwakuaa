    Laini Taylor

    Muse of Nightmares (2018)

    By the end of this book, I was just absolutely obsessed. I gave the duology over to Billy and I don't know if he'll ever read them in his life, but I really hope he gets around to them and tells me all about it. It was an amazing conclusion to an amazing story and was gripping till the very end. Can't say much since I don't really remember it, but it was a great series.

    Strange the Dreamer (2017)

    Ms. Clark gave this to either Yaw or Nimoh to give to me and I was super surprised that this was the level of material she recommended for me. I read it in the summer of 2021, and what I can really remember is staying up super late just reading and reading this book. The language and prose were addicting, and the plot was always becoming deeper and more intricate. I can say for certain that once I reached the end of the book, it was something that I never saw coming.

    I appreciate that this book was as much about Lazlo as it was about the other characters, and I thought it was especially interesting for its universe. I remember this book ending on the biggest loss possible — with our main girl with the butterflies dying after falling out of the island in the sky. All our other characters were simply great and I like that they all had their own little quirks which each helped the development of the story in their own way.
