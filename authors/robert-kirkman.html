    Invincible (2003)

    This was a pretty awesome series. A couple outdated elements in the earlier sections, but there were some generally really interesting dilemmas that were portrayed, and it was amazing to watch the characters get through them while trying to retain their morals. I find that struggle between the goals they set for themselves and what it takes to keep them the best parts about all of this in general.
