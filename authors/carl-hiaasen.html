    Carl Hiaasen

    Scat (2009)

    This book was fun. I don't think this is the first Hiaasen book I've read, but I largely went into this one because Nimoh gave it to me to read. Like all Hiaasen books that I can at least remember, this one was fun and interesting. They all center around a small cast of kids and eccentric adults and work to bring a no-good environmental-damaging person to justice.

    The only thing that seems to get me is how blunt he seems to be with some subjects, specifically danger and violence. Starch getting shot in the leg definitely came out of nowhere to me. But that's exactly what happened. It was a cool book though — Hiaasen is a great storyteller and I enjoy the casual jumping from different characters, which was really palpable especially for a kids' book.
